10/15/24: The Exam Week Struggle

This week has been intense! Three exams in four days is no joke. I spent the entire weekend in the library, surrounded by textbooks and coffee cups. The hardest part is staying focused when everyone around you is stressed too. But I discovered that taking short breaks every hour really helps. Walking around campus for 10 minutes clears my mind and I can study better afterwards.

10/22/24: Group Project Adventures

Working on a group project is always interesting. Today we had our first meeting for our Computer Science project. Everyone has different ideas and schedules, which makes coordination tricky. I volunteered to be the team leader because I like organizing things. We set up a shared document and created a timeline. Hopefully this will help us stay on track. The project is due in three weeks, so we need to work efficiently and support each other.

10/28/24: Finding Balance

Student life is all about balance. Between classes, assignments, and trying to have a social life, it feels like there are never enough hours in the day. Today I realized I need to prioritize better. I made a schedule where I dedicate specific hours to studying and leave some time for relaxing and hanging out with friends. Mental health is just as important as grades. Sometimes taking a break is the most productive thing you can do.
